questions & answers

Q: Will there be transportation to/from the ceremony?

A: Yes! Each hotel will have a school bus for ceremony transportation! Please click Transportation for details.

  Transportation

Q: I'm staying at the TownePlace, will there be transportation back to my hotel?

A: Also yes! A shuttle will make regular trips between the hotels starting at 9:30 and ending at 12:30.

Q: Is Minneapolis Lyft/Uber friendly?

A: You betch'ya.

Q: Where should I park?

A: If you're staying at the TownePlace please park there and use the shuttles for the ceremony & reception. Parking is limited at the Nicollet.

Q: Can I drive to the church on Saturday?

A: You can, however there is only street parking available. In addition, parking back at the Nicollet is very limited so if you're staying at the TownePlace please consider using the provided shuttles.

Q: How much time should I allow to drive to MSP Int'l Airport on Sunday?

A: The trip takes approximately 20 minutes, pending traffic. MSP is pretty good with security lines and checkin, but expect weekend/holiday crowds.
